Exploring Coffee Beans: Varieties and Flavors

1. Arabica

Known for its smooth, nuanced flavor profile, Arabica beans are the most popular and widely cultivated type of coffee beans. They typically have a mild acidity and notes of fruit, chocolate, or nuts, making them a favorite among coffee connoisseurs.
